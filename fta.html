Frequently asked questions

FTA (First Time Authors) community is a group or platform designed to support and connect authors who are navigating the process of writing, publishing, and marketing their first book.

Anyone who is writing or planning to write their first book can join! We are happy to have you in our community!

Members typically gain access to:
  • Writing tips and resources
  • Networking with other first-time authors
  • Opportunities for feedback and critiques
  • Guidance on publishing and marketing
